Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity is 100 cards for most products. However, we offer flexible quantities for different product types. Contact us for specific requirements.

How long does production take?

Standard orders typically take 5-7 business days from artwork approval. Rush orders can be completed in 2-3 business days for an additional fee.

Do you provide design services?

Yes, we offer free design support for all our products. Our in-house design team will help create card templates that perfectly represent your brand.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, CDR, and high-resolution PNG/JPG files. Our team will guide you on the best format for your specific design.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes, we deliver to all major cities across India. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location and order size.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, UPI payments, and major credit/debit cards. For bulk orders, we offer credit terms for verified institutions.
